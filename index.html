About

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Physics at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. My research interests are Raman Spectroscopy, Fullerides and PAHs. In particular, I focus on the changes of the properties, of these close and open structures of carbon, induced by external stimuli such as high pressure. I am a member of the Optics and Spectroscopy Laboratory of the Physics Department, AUTh.
