About

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Physics at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. My research interests are Raman Spectroscopy, Fullerides and PAHs, with a particular focus on the changes of their properties induced by external stimuli such as high pressure. I am a member of the Optics and Spectroscopy Laboratory of the Physics Department, AUTh.
